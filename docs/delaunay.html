Delaunay Triangulation

Application of a delaunay triangulation implemented for the discipline of data structure and algorithms at EMAp Master taught by Jorge Poco. An implementation of the algorithm was implemented with C++, to demonstrate the result, random points from an image (that can be from a edge or not) were selected and with the triangulation, each triangle will be coloured by the mean color of it's vertex.

The variations of the result can be selected below, there are two different pictures, the points were selected at random in the full image or at random in the edges of the images, the number of points can be selected, with a high number more details of the image are visualized and the colouring of triangles can be by the mean color of the vertex or by a interpolation to generate a gradient.
